Our Pillars

Vision

Our vision to combine thought leaders from diverse backgrounds and deep technology expertise has created a culture of business first and commitment to provide meaningful technology solutions, ensuring our engagements are exceedingly responsive and deliver true value. Our success is built on a strong foundation of empowering people and a clear strategic direction to help customers business succeed.

Roots

CHANGEPOND’s management team has a rich product development heritage and was part of a pioneering Software Product Venture. The team built a 3000 people product company and within 7 years took the company to IPO with $1Bn valuation in 1999. It was then one of the largest enterprise products to be built on Microsoft technologies and the first major ERP in the world with a Graphic User Interface (GUI). The core team has worked with marquee customers like Intel, Swatch, Boeing, SwissPost, FIFA, GE.

Competency

We deliver innovative solutions using our agile DevOps powered COVE™methodology, offering Technology Consulting, Experience Design and Technology Execution services including Enterprise Architecture, UI/UX, Digital Modernization, Independent Software Testing, Application & Infrastructure Management and Data Solutions & Analytics. Our teams in USA, UK, Europe and Asia support leading global organizations in the Financial Services, Education, Healthcare & Life-sciences and Manufacturing & Utilities business. With over 16 years of delivery excellence, CHANGEPOND is all about “Experiencing Trust and Lasting Solutions”.

Culture

Customer Focus

We have entered the era of the customer. Today, providing customers with outstanding service is essential to building loyal customers and a long lasting brand. It’s our job every day to make every important aspect of the customers experience a little bit better. Our people and methods are aligned to engage and provide differentiated value in every step of the engagement. We start with what the customer needs and prioritize our roadmap directly based on these insights. This has helped us to evolve our service and release features which we know in advance customer will love.

Passion

Doing things that you enjoy is better than doing things you don’t enjoy… right? And when you enjoy doing something you tend to not only does it stronger, better, faster, longer, etc., but you tend to get it done with a smile on your face. Everything we do at CHANGEPOND we do with passion — for our clients, our communities and our organization. Each associate has the drive and commitment to do whatever it takes to help our customers succeed and we apply that passion to help sustain our communities and our environment.

Collaboration

Who is giving the orders to ants? No one. They are self-organizing. Each of our immune systems get smarter over the years as its biochemical parts share information, and it responds with individualized defenses, but it isn’t conscious and it has no memory. A cornerstone of CHANGEPOND’s success is the interconnectivity of our associates and teams across business units. We believe that the better we share knowledge and work together, the more we can achieve for our clients and ourselves. We strive to maintain the culture in which everyone is a hands-on contributor and feels comfortable sharing ideas and opinions.

Empowerment

The key to realizing a dream is to focus not on success but on significance--and then even the small steps and little victories along your path will take on greater meaning. We deﬁne ourselves by our ability to deliver results. That means taking the initiative to ﬁnd new ways to get the job done. We encourage end-to-end ownership, responsibility, accountability and recognition. Our belief is that if you get the culture right, most of the other stuff, like great customer service, or building a great long-term brand, or empowering passionate employees and customers, will happen on its own.

Transparency

Transparency isn’t about knowing everyone’s business, it’s about making sure everyone has the information they need to do their jobs effectively. CHANGEPOND is built on a foundation of open and honest communication. We believe the only way to ensure success for our clients and ourselves is to operate in a fully transparent manner. We ensure that everyone in the company knows where to turn when they need information and share feedback that can make us a better and stronger.

Integrity

Integrity starts with honesty, decency and trustworthiness. Following through with your word and being impeccable and honorable with your actions creates respect and professionalism. We never compromise on integrity and we take every decision accordingly. Integrity also means that we treat our colleagues and clients with respect and value their opinions. We act with “Do what is right, let the consequence follow”.

Quality Management

To ensure the highest possible quality of software development and maintenance, we have devised a CHANGEPOND Quality Management Process (CQMS) that emphasizes problem prevention rather than problem correction. Our CQMS integrates our quality approach throughout the software development life cycle, thereby ensuring that quality is built in as development progresses.

The CHANGEPOND Quality Management System (CQMS) is assessed and certified by TUV Rheinland in accordance with the ISO 9001:2008 standards.

Continuing our journey to strive and keep up our excellence in quality, we have embarked on a voyage to attain CMMI Level 4 & 5 certification having reached CMMI Level 3.

Leadership Program

by Changepond  Posted on 04 Mar, 2015 at 10:00 AM

Changepond Technologies with the help of YUSHU Consulting promoted by a team of senior IT industry leaders conducts an in-house Leadership Program providing Changeponders the opportunity to take the lead and excel. The program is based on a framework of integrating the concepts of strategic development with the day-to-day activities of Business/Delivery leaders.

The program focuses on the bringing about the following transformations:

  • Managers Into Leaders (Differentiating traditional management styles and true leadership)
  • Embedding Change (Equip Changeponders with methods and tools to implement long-term change)
  • Creating Mission, Vision and Values (Build frameworks to support success and growth)
  • Self Managed Teams (Focus on skills and motivation for self management)
